Destinations in kudla!

Panambur Beach

One of the safest and most visited beaches in Mangalore…

Panambur Beach is known for water sports, camel rides, and beautiful sunsets. It is clean, family-friendly, and has food stalls along the shore.

Tannirbhavi Beach

Calm, clean, and surrounded by casuarina trees…

A peaceful beach offering stunning sunsets and a wooden walkway. Accessible by road or ferry from Sultan Battery.

Someshwara Beach

Famous for “Rudra Shile” rock formations…

Someshwara Beach offers magnificent rock formations and a historic Shiva temple. Swimming is unsafe due to strong currents.

Pilikula Nisargadhama

Nature, heritage village & zoo.

Pilikula Nisargadhama is a large eco-educational nature park in Mangalore. It includes a zoo, botanical garden, lake, science center, and heritage village. It’s a peaceful destination for nature lovers, families, and students.

Sultan Battery

A historic watchtower built by Tipu Sultan…

Sultan Battery offers river views and ferry rides to Tannirbhavi Beach.

Kadri Park

The biggest park in Mangalore with gardens and toy train…

A beautiful place for children and families with a toy train, flowers, and walking tracks.
